interesting facts about camels

  1. Camel's ears are furry

interesting facts about Chameleons

  1. Chameleons can change colors depending on their mood and surroundings
  2. Chameleons are reptiles
  3. Their tongue is a very fast and sticky projectile used to catch food.

interesting facts about Pandas

  1. Pandas are only found in China
  2. Pandas are very picky eaters
  3. They are not actually bears
